Frequently Asked Questions

Clear your doubts about how to list and monetize your space on SeatHub

How much does it cost to list my space?

It's completely free! You only pay a small commission when you receive bookings. No registration fees, monthly fees or hidden costs.

Can I set my own prices?

Yes! You have full control over prices, operating hours and availability. You can adjust values by time, day of the week or time of year.

What types of spaces can I list?

We accept coworking, meeting rooms, private offices, event spaces, phone booths and any space that can be used for work.

How can I manage my bookings?

Through our web dashboard or mobile app. You receive instant notifications of new bookings and can manage everything in real time.
